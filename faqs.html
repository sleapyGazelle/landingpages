What does an Eden Health membership include?

Your membership includes all virtual encounters (messaging, care coordination, virtual doctor appointments, etc.), so you never have to pay anything extra when speaking with one of our providers via the app. If you visit us in person, though, your typical copay (if you have one) will apply, and any services will be run through your insurance, just like you would experience at any other doctor's office.

‌

Who can use my Eden Health membership?

Your membership includes you, your spouse, and any dependents age 18+.

‌

Is Eden Health health an insurance company or does it replace my existing insurance?

Nope! We're not a health insurance carrier. We are a virtual and in-person primary care provider that integrates with any existing plans you have. We accept most major health insurance carriers.

‌

Does Eden Health take my insurance?

We work with most insurance plans. We won't bill you or your insurance for any virtual services. However, if you need care from an outside provider, Eden will work with you to find in-network referrals to labs and specialists.

‌

What if I already have a primary care doctor or specialist that you see regularly?

That’s great! We complement any existing benefits or providers that you already have. Feel free to message us with any questions about physical or mental health symptoms, benefits or prescriptions, or specialist referrals. For example, if you need a prescription refill, you can message a provider via our app who can send it to your pharmacy 24/7.

‌

What if I already have my benefits package finalized for next year — how can I use Eden Health?

We complement any existing benefits packages that you already have. You can still use Eden Health at any time, regardless of your current benefits.

‌

Are Eden Health providers contracted?

No, all of our providers are full-time employees of Eden Health.

‌

How much does Eden Health cost per member?

Your sponsor has fully paid for your membership. The only time you may incur an additional cost is if you visit us in person.

  • Virtual Primary Care: $0
  • Insurance Navigation: $0
  • Care Coordination: $0
  • Care at Clinics: Copay + billed to insurance

‌

How secure is my personal health information?

As a healthcare practice, Eden Health is a covered entity under The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 ("HIPAA"). All of your protected health information is encrypted while at rest using current industry-standard encryption.

‌

Does Eden Health report anything to my employer or office building?

No, everything is confidential. Eden Health will never share your personal health information with anyone — including your employer or office building — without your consent.
